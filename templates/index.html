API Access

Access all templates programmatically through our REST API. Perfect for automation and integration with your existing tools.

Available Endpoints:
  • GET /api/templates - All templates
  • GET /api/templates/{category} - Templates by category
  • POST /render - Render template with parameters
  • POST /upload - Upload new template
  • GET /search?q=query - Search templates
  • GET /health - Health check
